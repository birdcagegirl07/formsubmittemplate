About Me

Hi there, thanks for stopping by. My name is Chloe and I am a web and ux designer, passionate about how strategy and design can be used to solve problems. I was once a psychology degree dropout, but moved the problem solving from a therapist’s office to a computer screen, where user interface and experience design has been my focus.

I graduated from the 2020 class of Curtin University with my B.A in Digital Design. My studies focused heavily on user’s interaction with technology, how technology has interweaved with our life, and how we can design digital solutions to solve day to day problems.

I have also grown an interest in front-end development, and have a solid understanding of html, css and css frameworks including Bootstrap and Foundation. I am always looking to grow my skillset, and am currently undertaking a Javascript course through Code Academy to further my coding knowledge.

If you think we would work well together, I’d love to hear from you.
